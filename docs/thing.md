Mental Model Trainer (Brain Gym)

System Design Questions

Test user on questions or scenarios 
Creates new scenarios or questions based on web search

User can vote if question is good or not (keep in database or discard)

Goa: over time train the users mental model to be self reliant and knowledgeable on different topics
and what to do in certain situations, their expertise and engineering judgement. 

In an age where AI is the primary tool to push forward at the cost of learning on the job.
Mental gyms seem like the natural progression of the physical counterpart.
